<p><p>Vascular graft devices market includes various types such as Aortic grafts, Dialysis grafts, Peripheral grafts, Vascular patches, Tunneling devices, and accessories. Aortic grafts are used for aortic aneurysms, whereas Dialysis grafts are used for hemodialysis procedures. Peripheral grafts are used to bypass blockages in peripheral arteries. Vascular patches are used for sealing vessels after surgeries. Tunneling devices are used for creating subcutaneous pathways for graft placement. Lastly, accessories include tools and instruments necessary for graft implantation and maintenance.</p></p>
