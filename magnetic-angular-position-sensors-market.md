These sensors are commonly used in applications where the position needs to be tracked within a limited range of motion. On the other hand, sensors with a 0-360° sensing range are capable of measuring full-circle angular displacement, providing complete information on the position of an object within a full rotation. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
