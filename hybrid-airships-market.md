<p><p>Hybrid Airships can be classified into two main market types based on the type of wings they have: Fixed Wings and Rotary Wings. Fixed Wing hybrid airships have wings that are fixed in position and provide lift through forward motion. They are typically used for long-distance travel and heavy cargo transportation. On the other hand, Rotary Wing hybrid airships have rotating wings that provide lift and propulsion. They are more maneuverable and can hover in place, making them ideal for search and rescue operations and military applications.</p></p>
