<p><p>Shaft-mounted gearboxes are classified into different types based on the arrangement of shafts and gears. Parallel shaft gearboxes have input and output shafts that are parallel to each other, offering high power transmission efficiency. Staggered shaft gearboxes have input and output shafts arranged at an angle, providing compact size and efficient power transfer. Other types of shaft-mounted gearboxes may include right angle gearboxes, worm gearboxes, and planetary gearboxes, each designed for specific applications and performance requirements. These different types cater to various industries and applications based on their unique features and benefits.</p></p>
