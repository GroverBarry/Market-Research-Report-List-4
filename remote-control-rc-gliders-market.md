<p><p>Remote control (RC) gliders market can be categorized into two types: Motivated and Unpowered. Motivated RC gliders are powered by an engine or motor, allowing users to control the speed and direction of the glider. On the other hand, unpowered RC gliders rely solely on wind and thermal currents for flight, requiring skill and technique from the operator to maintain altitude and steer the glider. Both types offer unique challenges and experiences for enthusiasts of the hobby.</p></p>
