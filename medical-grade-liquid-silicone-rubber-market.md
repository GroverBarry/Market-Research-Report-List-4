<p><p>Medical-grade liquid silicone rubber is categorized into three different market types based on the duration of exposure to human tissue or bodily fluids. Limited exposure refers to applications where the material comes into contact with the body for a short period. Prolonged exposure involves longer periods of contact with human tissue. Permanent contact market involves applications where the material is in continuous contact with the body. These distinctions help manufacturers and healthcare professionals choose the right type of silicone rubber for specific medical devices and components.</p></p>
