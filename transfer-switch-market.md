<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic transfer switches are equipped with sensors that can detect when the power supply from the primary source fails, automatically switching the power source to a backup generator or alternative power source. On the other hand, manual transfer switches require the user to physically switch between power sources in the event of a power outage. Both types of transfer switches provide a reliable solution for ensuring continuous power supply in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
