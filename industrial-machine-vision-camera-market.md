<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial machine vision cameras are categorized into two main types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras are designed to capture images by scanning a subject line by line, making them ideal for inspecting continuous objects like conveyor belts. On the other hand, area scan cameras capture a full image of an object at once, making them suitable for still objects or small batches of products. Both types offer advanced features such as high resolution, fast processing, and precise imaging capabilities to meet the demanding requirements of industrial applications.</p></p>
