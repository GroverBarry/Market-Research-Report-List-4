<p><ul><li>Wired Temperature Monitoring</li><li>Wireless Temperature Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigeration monitoring can be categorized into two main types: wired temperature monitoring and wireless temperature monitoring. Wired temperature monitoring involves using physical cables to connect sensors and monitors, providing real-time data on the temperature of the refrigeration unit. On the other hand, wireless temperature monitoring utilizes wireless technology to transmit data from sensors to a central monitoring system, offering the flexibility of monitoring temperatures remotely and in real-time. Both types serve the purpose of ensuring proper refrigeration temperature control and preventing food spoilage.</p></p>
