<p><ul><li>Choke</li><li>Flanges</li><li>Hangers</li><li>Master Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wellhead system market consists of various types such as choke, flanges, hangers, master valve, and others. Choke valves are used to control the flow of fluids in the wellhead system. Flanges provide a connection point between different components of the system. Hangers support the weight of the tubing and casing in the well. Master valves are used to shut off the flow of fluids in case of emergencies. Other components in the market include tubing heads, casing heads, and other accessories essential for the operation of the wellhead system.</p></p>
