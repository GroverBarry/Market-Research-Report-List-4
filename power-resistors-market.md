<p><strong>The Power Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chassis Mounted</li><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Through-Hole Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power resistors are available in various market types to suit different applications. Chassis mounted resistors are designed to be mounted directly onto the chassis of electronic equipment for easy installation and heat dissipation. Surface mounted resistors are soldered directly onto the circuit board, saving space and reducing assembly time. Through-hole mounted resistors have leads that are inserted through holes in the circuit board and soldered on the opposite side for a secure connection. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
