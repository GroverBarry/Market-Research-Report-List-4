<p><p>Fuse holders are devices that hold fuses, protecting electrical circuits from overloads. There are two main types of fuse holders: open fuse holders and fully enclosed fuse holders. Open fuse holders are open-ended and allow for easy access to the fuse, making them ideal for quick replacement. Fully enclosed fuse holders, on the other hand, provide more protection and are recommended for applications where exposure to the fuse needs to be minimized. Both types of fuse holders are essential components in electrical systems to ensure safety and proper functioning.</p></p>
