<p><p>Bio plastic packaging market can be segmented into different types based on the materials used. Starch blends are derived from natural sources such as corn, potatoes, and wheat, making them biodegradable and compostable. PLA (polylactic acid) is made from renewable resources like corn starch or sugarcane and is compostable in industrial facilities. PBAT (polybutylene adipate terephthalate) is a biodegradable polyester that can decompose in both industrial and home composting conditions. Other bio plastic packaging materials include cellulose, PHAs, and bio-based PET.</p></p>
