<p><p>Biological catalyst market consists of different types such as Oxidoreductases, Transferases, Hydrolases, Isomerases, Lyases, and Ligases. Oxidoreductases catalyze oxidation-reduction reactions, Transferases transfer functional groups between molecules, Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, Isomerases catalyze isomerization reactions, Lyases catalyze addition or removal of functional groups to form double bonds, and Ligases catalyze the joining of two molecules using ATP. Each type of biological catalyst plays a crucial role in various biochemical processes, making them essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
