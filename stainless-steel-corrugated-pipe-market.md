<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Corrugated Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Stainless steel corrugated pipes come in different types based on the connection method. Flange connection involves using flanges to connect the pipes, providing a secure and leak-proof joint. Welding involves permanently joining the pipes using heat and pressure, ensuring a strong bond. Screw connection utilizes screws to connect the pipes quickly and easily. Other types, such as compression fittings or clamp connections, offer alternative ways to secure the pipes. Each connection type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application and requirements.</p></p>
