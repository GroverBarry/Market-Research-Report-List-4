<p><p>Automotive bolt (fastener) market consists of two types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, like screws and nuts, have spiral grooves that allow them to be easily screwed into place. On the other hand, non-threaded fasteners, such as clips and pins, do not have threads and are typically used for holding components together or aligning parts. Both types are essential in the automotive industry for securing various parts and components in vehicles.</p></p>
