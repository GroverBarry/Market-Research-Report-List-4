<p><p>Electrical tapes are widely used for insulation and protection in electrical wiring applications. Cloth electrical tape is made of woven cotton fabric coated with a rubber adhesive, providing good flexibility and conformability. PVC electrical tape is made of polyvinyl chloride and is known for its durability and resistance to moisture and chemicals. PET electrical tape is made of polyester film, offering high temperature resistance and excellent electrical insulation properties. Each type of tape caters to specific needs in the electrical industry.</p></p>
