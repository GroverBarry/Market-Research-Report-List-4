<p><p>Choledochoscope market is segmented into two types: rigid choledochoscope and flexible choledochoscope. Rigid choledochoscopes are rigid, straight instruments used for direct visualization of the bile ducts, often preferred for diagnostic procedures. On the other hand, flexible choledochoscopes are more maneuverable and can access hard-to-reach areas within the bile ducts, making them suitable for therapeutic procedures. Both types play a crucial role in the diagnosis and treatment of various biliary disorders, catering to the diverse needs of healthcare providers.</p></p>
