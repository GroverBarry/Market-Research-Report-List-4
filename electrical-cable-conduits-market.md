<p><p>Electrical cable conduits are used to protect and route electrical wiring in buildings and infrastructure. There are two main types of conduits: flexible conduits and rigid conduits. Flexible conduits are made of materials like PVC or metal and are able to bend and twist, making them ideal for areas where the wiring may need to move. Rigid conduits, on the other hand, are made of materials like steel or aluminum and provide a more stable and protective barrier for wiring that needs to remain in a fixed position.</p></p>
