<p><p>Medical radiation detection market is segmented into gas-filled detectors, scintillators, and solid-state detectors. Gas-filled detectors such as ionization chamber and proportional counters are commonly used for detecting radiation in medical imaging. Scintillators are used in gamma cameras and PET scanners to detect emitted photons. Solid-state detectors like silicon and germanium detectors offer high energy resolution and are used in radiation therapy and nuclear medicine. Each type of detector plays a crucial role in detecting and measuring radiation in the medical field.</p></p>
