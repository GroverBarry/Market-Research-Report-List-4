<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Linear Friction Welding</li><li>Friction Stir Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Friction welding equipment market consists of various types including rotary friction welding, linear friction welding, and friction stir welding. Rotary friction welding involves one part rotating while the other is kept stationary, creating friction and heat for joining. Linear friction welding utilizes linear motion to generate heat and create welds. Friction stir welding involves a non-consumable rotating tool that creates friction between materials, joining them together. Each type of friction welding offers unique advantages and applications in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
