<p><p>Textile Fluorine-free Water Repellence technology is available in three main market types: weak cationic, weakly anionic, and non-ionic. Weak cationic repellents have a positive charge, which helps in better adhesion to textiles. Weakly anionic repellents have a negative charge and provide good repellency properties. Non-ionic repellents do not have any charge and are versatile in their application. Each market type offers different benefits and can be chosen based on the specific requirements of the textile and its intended use.</p></p>
