<p><p>Embedded computers are classified into four main market types based on their processor architectures: ARM, X86, PowerPC, and others. ARM processors are known for their energy efficiency and low cost, making them popular in mobile devices and IoT applications. X86 processors are commonly found in industrial automation and networking equipment due to their high performance capabilities. PowerPC processors are used in automotive and aerospace industries for their reliability and processing power. Other processors like MIPS and RISC-V are used in niche markets such as consumer electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
