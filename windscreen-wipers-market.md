<p><p>Windscreen wipers market can be classified into two types: boneless wipers and bone wipers. Boneless wipers are designed without the traditional metal frame, offering a sleeker look and improved performance by maintaining constant pressure on the windshield. On the other hand, bone wipers have a metal frame that provides stability and durability. Both types are essential for ensuring visibility during rainy or snowy conditions, but boneless wipers are preferred for their modern design and efficiency, while bone wipers are known for their reliability.</p></p>
