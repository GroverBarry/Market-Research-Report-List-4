<p><p>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) CO2 sensors are commonly available in two market types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel NDIR CO2 sensors utilize one infrared light source and detector to measure CO2 concentrations. On the other hand, Dual Channel NDIR CO2 sensors employ two light sources and detectors, allowing for more accurate and reliable measurements. Single Channel sensors are more cost-effective and suitable for simple applications, while Dual Channel sensors are preferred for applications requiring higher accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
