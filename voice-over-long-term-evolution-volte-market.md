<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CFSB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) market types include Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS), which enables voice calls over LTE networks; Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB), which switches to 3G or 2G networks for voice calls; Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE), which uses separate radios for voice and data; Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA), which allows voice calls over Wi-Fi networks; and Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC), which ensures seamless voice call handover between LTE and 3G networks.</p></p>
