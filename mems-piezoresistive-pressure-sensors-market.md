<p><p>MEMS piezoresistive pressure sensors are categorized into three main types based on their functionality. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure in relation to a vacuum reference. Relative (gauge) pressure sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Differential pressure sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points. These sensors are widely used in various industries for applications such as automotive, industrial, and medical devices to accurately measure and monitor pressure levels. Each type of sensor offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
