<p><p>District heating pipeline network market can be classified into two main types: pre-insulated steel and polymer. Pre-insulated steel pipelines are made of steel pipes covered with insulating material, providing durability and strength. On the other hand, polymer pipelines are made of plastic materials, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types of pipelines are commonly used in district heating systems to transport hot water or steam for heating purposes. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
