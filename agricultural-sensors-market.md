Humidity sensors measure the amount of moisture in the air or soil. Temperature sensors monitor temperature variations in the environment. Pressure sensors detect changes in air pressure, helping in weather monitoring. Other types of sensors in the agricultural market include pH sensors, light sensors, and nutrient sensors, which provide important data for crop management and precision farming.</p></p>
