<p><p>IR windows are used to protect sensors and detectors in infrared devices. They come in two main types - crystal material and polymer material. Crystal material IR windows are made from materials like sapphire or germanium, which provide high durability and optical clarity. Polymer material IR windows are made from materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, which are more cost-effective but may have lower optical quality. Both types are important for protecting and maintaining the performance of infrared devices in various applications.</p></p>
