<p><p>The car air filter market consists of different types, including paper and oil bath filters. Paper filters are made of pleated paper material that traps dirt and debris, while oil bath filters use a bath of oil to trap particles. Both types work by preventing contaminants from entering the engine and causing damage. Paper filters are more commonly used in modern vehicles due to their efficiency and ease of maintenance, while oil bath filters are seen in older or off-road vehicles for their durability and reusability.</p></p>
