<p><p>The Diving Goggles market can be categorized into three main types: Traditional Diving Mask, Over-ear, and Full Face goggles. Traditional Diving Masks are the most common type, covering the eyes and nose with a strap around the back of the head. Over-ear goggles have a similar design but with straps that go over the ears for added stability. Full Face goggles cover the entire face, providing a wider field of vision and allowing for easier breathing through both the nose and mouth.</p></p>
