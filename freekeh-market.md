This type of Freekeh retains all the nutrients and fiber of the wheat kernel, making it a very healthy choice. </p><p>Cracked Freekeh, on the other hand, is wholegrain Freekeh that has been cracked into smaller pieces for quicker cooking. This type of Freekeh still retains most of its nutrients and fiber, but may have a slightly different texture compared to wholegrain Freekeh. Both types are popular choices in the market for their health benefits and versatility in cooking.</p></p>
