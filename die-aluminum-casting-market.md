<p><strong>The Die Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-chamber Die Casting</li><li>Cold-chamber Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die Aluminum Casting Market Types include Hot-chamber Die Casting and Cold-chamber Die Casting. Hot-chamber Die Casting involves injecting molten metal into the mold cavity under high pressure, suitable for metals with lower melting points. In contrast, Cold-chamber Die Casting involves a separate furnace to melt the metal before being transferred to the injection chamber, ideal for metals with higher melting points. Both methods are commonly used in the manufacturing industry to produce intricate and high-quality aluminum castings for various applications.</p></p>
