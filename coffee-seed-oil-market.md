<p><p>Coffee Seed Oil is extracted in two main market types: Cold Pressed Oils and CO2/Solvent Extraction Oils. Cold pressed oils are derived from grinding coffee beans at low temperatures, preserving the natural properties and aroma of the oil. CO2/Solvent Extraction Oils involve using either carbon dioxide or solvents to extract the oil from the coffee beans. This method is more efficient but may alter the flavor and nutrients of the oil. Both market types offer unique benefits and are preferred by different consumers based on their preferences.</p></p>
