<p><p>The optical spectroscope market is divided into two main types: visible optic and invisible optic. Visible optic spectrometers are designed to measure wavelengths within the visible spectrum, allowing for analysis of colors and light sources. Invisible optic spectrometers, on the other hand, can detect wavelengths beyond the visible spectrum, such as infrared and ultraviolet light. Both types of spectrometers are used in a variety of industries for applications such as chemical analysis, environmental monitoring, and materials testing.</p></p>
