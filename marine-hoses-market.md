<p><strong>The Marine Hoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expandable Hoses</li><li>Porous or Perforated Hoses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Expandable Hoses are designed to expand when filled with water and contract when the water is turned off, making them lightweight and easy to maneuver. They are popular for boats and marine applications due to their flexibility and space-saving design. Porous or Perforated Hoses are designed with small holes along the length of the hose, allowing water to seep out slowly and evenly. These hoses are commonly used in marine applications for watering plants or washing decks without causing pooling or runoff.</p></p>
