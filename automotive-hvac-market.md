<p><p>The manual HVAC system requires the driver or passengers to manually adjust the temperature and airflow settings in the vehicle. It is a more traditional and cost-effective option compared to automatic HVAC systems. On the other hand, automatic HVAC systems use sensors to detect the temperature inside the vehicle and automatically adjust the settings to maintain a comfortable environment. This type of system is more convenient and provides a more consistent temperature throughout the vehicle. Both manual and automatic HVAC systems cater to different preferences and needs of consumers in the automotive market.</p></p>
