<p><p>Mannosylerythritol Lipids (MELs) are a type of natural biosurfactant produced by yeast fermentation. There are two main market types of MELs - MEL-A and MEL-B. MEL-A has a higher surface activity and lower critical micelle concentration, making it suitable for applications such as cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, MEL-B has a higher hydrophobicity and is more suitable for industrial uses like oil recovery and emulsification. Both types of MELs have unique properties that make them valuable in various markets.</p></p>
