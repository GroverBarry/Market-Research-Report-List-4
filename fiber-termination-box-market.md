<p><p>Fiber termination boxes are available in two main types: wall mount and rock mount. Wall mount fiber termination boxes are designed to be installed on walls, making them suitable for indoor settings where space is limited. On the other hand, rock mount fiber termination boxes are designed to be mounted on racks or cabinets, making them ideal for larger scale installations in data centers or telecommunication networks. Both types offer secure and organized termination points for fiber optic cables, ensuring efficient and reliable connectivity.</p></p>
