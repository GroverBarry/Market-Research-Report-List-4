<p><strong>The Coal Dewatering Centrifuges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Coal Dewatering Centrifuges</li><li>Horizontal Coal Dewatering Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coal dewatering centrifuges are used to separate water from coal slurry in the mining industry. Two main types are vertical and horizontal centrifuges. Vertical centrifuges rotate on a vertical axis, causing the solids to settle towards the walls of the centrifuge, while horizontal centrifuges rotate on a horizontal axis, allowing for a more controlled separation process. Both types of centrifuges are crucial in efficiently drying coal and reducing moisture content for transportation and storage.</p></p>
