<p><p>The automotive pinion gear market includes different types such as bevel gear and helical gear. Bevel gears have cone-shaped teeth and are mainly used in applications requiring high speed and power transmission, like differential gear systems in vehicles. Helical gears have angled teeth and provide smoother and quieter operation compared to straight-cut gears. They are commonly found in automotive transmissions due to their efficiency and ability to handle heavy loads. Both types play crucial roles in the automotive industry for various applications.</p></p>
