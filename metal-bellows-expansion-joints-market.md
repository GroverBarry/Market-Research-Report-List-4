<p><strong>The Metal Bellows Expansion Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal bellows expansion joints are classified into two types in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I expansion joints are designed for applications where axial movement is required, such as in pipelines or ducting systems. These joints can absorb thermal expansion and contraction, as well as vibration. On the other hand, Type II expansion joints are used in applications that require lateral movement, such as in turbulent flow systems or where misalignment may occur. Both types of expansion joints are essential in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of various industrial systems.</p></p>
