<p><p>PC battery market consists of three main types: Nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries, Nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) batteries, and Lithium batteries. Nickel-cadmium batteries are known for their durability and resistance to overcharging. NiMH batteries have higher energy densities and longer lifespans compared to Ni-Cd batteries. Lithium batteries are the most popular choice for PCs due to their lightweight design and high energy capacity. They are also known for their long lifespan, high efficiency, and ability to hold a charge for longer periods.</p></p>
