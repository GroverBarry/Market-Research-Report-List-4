<p><p>Road Asphalt market consists of two main types: Petroleum Asphalt and Natural Asphalt. Petroleum Asphalt is derived from crude oil refining process and is widely used in road construction due to its high durability and versatility. On the other hand, Natural Asphalt, also known as rock asphalt or Gilsonite, is a naturally occurring asphalt found in certain areas around the world. It is known for its high melting point and water resistance, making it suitable for various road construction applications. Both types play a crucial role in the road construction industry.</p></p>
