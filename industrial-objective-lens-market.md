<p><p>Industrial objective lenses vary in magnification levels to suit different applications and specifications. Scanning objective lenses at 4x magnification are used for quickly scanning a large area with lower resolution. Low power objective lenses at 10x magnification provide better detail for general observation tasks. High power objective lenses at 40x magnification are ideal for more detailed inspection and analysis. Oil immersion objective lenses at 100x magnification offer the highest level of resolution by using oil to reduce light refraction, making them suitable for advanced microscopy applications.</p></p>
