<p><p>Trencher market is divided into two main types: Wheel Trenchers and Chain Trenchers. Wheel Trenchers are suitable for lighter, less rocky soil conditions and are more maneuverable, making them ideal for small to medium construction projects. On the other hand, Chain Trenchers are best suited for hard and compacted soil, rocky terrain, and larger construction sites due to their powerful digging capabilities. Both types of trenchers serve different purposes and cater to specific soil conditions in the construction industry.</p></p>
