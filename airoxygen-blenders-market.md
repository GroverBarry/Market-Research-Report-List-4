<p><p>Air/Oxygen Blenders are medical devices used to deliver precise oxygen concentrations to patients. They are categorized into Low-flow (0-3 lpm bleed flow), Mid-flow (3-6 lpm bleed flow), and High-flow (6-12 lpm and above) types based on the rate at which oxygen is delivered. Low-flow blenders are suitable for patients requiring lower oxygen concentrations, while high-flow blenders are used for patients needing higher oxygen levels. The mid-flow blenders fall in between, catering to patients with moderate oxygen requirements.</p></p>
