<p><p>Holographic diffraction gratings are used in a variety of industries for their ability to disperse light into its component wavelengths. There are two main types of holographic gratings: plane type and concave type. Plane type holographic gratings are flat and are used for general spectroscopy applications. Concave type holographic gratings have a curved surface, allowing for higher resolution and better focusing capabilities, making them ideal for applications such as astronomy and laser systems. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the desired outcome.</p></p>
