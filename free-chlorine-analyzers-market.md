<p><p>Free chlorine analyzers are devices used to measure the concentration of free chlorine in water. Two common types of analyzers are X-ray Fluorescence and UV Fluorescence. </p><p>X-ray Fluorescence analyzers utilize the emission of fluorescent X-rays to determine the chlorine concentration. UV Fluorescence analyzers, on the other hand, use ultraviolet light to induce fluorescence in the free chlorine molecules, allowing for measurement.</p><p>Both types of analyzers are effective in providing accurate and reliable results for monitoring free chlorine levels in water treatment processes.</p></p>
