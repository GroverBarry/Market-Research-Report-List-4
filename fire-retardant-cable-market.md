<p><p>Fire retardant cables are categorized into two main types - single core cables and multicore cables. Single core cables consist of a single conductor surrounded by insulation and a sheath, typically used in applications where high voltage transmission is required. On the other hand, multicore cables contain multiple conductors within the same cable, allowing for the transmission of multiple signals or power sources simultaneously. Both types of cables are designed to inhibit the spread of fire and smoke in the event of a fire, making them crucial for ensuring the safety of buildings and occupants.</p></p>
