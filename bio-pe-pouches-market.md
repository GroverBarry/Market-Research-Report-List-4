<p><p>Bio PE pouches are a sustainable packaging solution made from renewable resources. They come in three main types based on the filling process: hot fill, cold fill, and ambient fill. Hot fill pouches are used for products that are filled at high temperatures, while cold fill pouches are filled at lower temperatures. Ambient fill pouches are used for products that do not require heating or cooling during filling. These different types cater to various packaging needs across industries while offering eco-friendly and recyclable packaging options.</p></p>
