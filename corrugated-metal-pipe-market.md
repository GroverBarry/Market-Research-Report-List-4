<p><strong>The Corrugated Metal Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated metal pipe markets are typically categorized into Type I and Type II based on their performance and durability standards. Type I pipes are designed for use in areas with limited cover and low traffic volume, providing basic protection against erosion and structural damage. On the other hand, Type II pipes are more robust, offering enhanced strength and resistance to heavy loads and high-velocity flow. They are commonly used in areas with higher cover and heavy traffic volume, ensuring long-term reliability and performance.</p></p>
