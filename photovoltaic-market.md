<p><p>Photovoltaic market can be segmented into three main types: Thin Film, Mono Si, and Multi Si. Thin Film technology involves depositing a thin layer of semiconductor material onto a substrate, making it lightweight and flexible. Mono Si panels are made from a single crystal structure, offering high efficiency and durability. Multi Si panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, balancing efficiency and cost. Each market type has its own advantages and is utilized in various applications depending on factors such as performance requirements, cost considerations, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
