<p><p>Waterborne polyurethane paint is available in different types such as polyurethane emulsion coating, polyurethane dispersion coating, and polyurethane aqueous solution coating. Polyurethane emulsion coating is a waterborne coating where polyurethane particles are dispersed in water. Polyurethane dispersion coating involves polyurethane particles suspended in water. Polyurethane aqueous solution coating is a waterborne coating where polyurethane is dissolved in water. These different types cater to various applications and provide different levels of durability and performance.</p></p>
