<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) and Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) are both types of high-performance thermoplastics known for their exceptional transparency, chemical resistance, and durability. COP is a homopolymer, consisting of repeating units of cyclic olefin, while COC is a copolymer made from a mixture of cyclic olefin and other monomers. Both materials are widely used in various industries such as packaging, medical devices, electronics, and automotive due to their unique combination of properties.</p></p>
