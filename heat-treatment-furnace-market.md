<p><p>Heat treatment furnace market can be divided into two main types: atmosphere furnaces and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces utilize controlled gas atmospheres to heat treat materials, allowing for specific heat treatment results. Vacuum furnaces, on the other hand, operate in a high vacuum environment, preventing oxidation and contamination during the heat treating process. Both types of furnaces are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for precise and consistent heat treatment of materials.</p></p>
