<p><p>ABS sensors are crucial components in modern vehicles for measuring wheel speed and detecting wheel lock-up during braking. There are two main types of ABS sensors: Hall type and magnetic/electric type. Hall type sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel speed, while magnetic/electric type sensors use a combination of magnetic and electric fields. Both types are widely used in the automotive industry and play a key role in ensuring the effectiveness of anti-lock braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
