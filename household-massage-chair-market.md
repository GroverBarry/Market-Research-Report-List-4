<p><p>Household massage chairs can be categorized into two main types: Rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs have a track mechanism that allows the massage rollers to move up and down along a set path, providing targeted and precise massages. On the other hand, trackless massage chairs use advanced technology to provide more flexibility and customization in massage options without the need for a track. Both types offer unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers in the household massage chair market.</p></p>
