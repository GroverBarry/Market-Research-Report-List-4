<p><p>Clear coatings market consists of different types such as solvent-borne, water-borne, powder coating, and other coatings. Solvent-borne coatings are traditional coatings that contain volatile organic compounds. Water-borne coatings are environmentally-friendly alternatives to solvent-based coatings. Powder coatings are cured using heat to form a hard finish. Other coatings may include UV-curable coatings, high solids coatings, and radiation-curable coatings. Each type of clear coating offers unique characteristics and benefits to cater to various application needs in industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
