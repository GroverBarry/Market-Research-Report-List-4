<p><strong>The Large Size Laser Processing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Processing Machine</li><li>Fiber Laser Processing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large size laser processing machines are divided into two main types: CO2 laser processing machines and fiber laser processing machines. CO2 laser processing machines use a gas mixture to create the laser beam, making them suitable for cutting and engraving materials like wood, acrylic, and plastic. On the other hand, fiber laser processing machines use optical fibers to generate the laser beam, making them more efficient for cutting metal materials like steel and aluminum. Both types of machines cater to specific market needs based on material and application requirements.</p></p>
