<p><p>The power tool motor market is segmented into two main types: DC motor and AC motor. On the other hand, AC motors are typically used in corded power tools as they are easier to maintain and operate at a higher power output. Both types of motors have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific application and requirements of the power tool.</p></p>
