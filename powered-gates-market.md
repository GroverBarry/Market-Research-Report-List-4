<p><p>Powered gates market can be categorized into various types such as switch, ECU (Electronic Control Unit), latch, motor/actuator, and relay. Switches are used to control the movement of the gate, while ECUs regulate the overall operation of the gate system. Latches secure the gate in place when closed, and motors/actuators are responsible for the physical movement of the gate. Relays provide the necessary power to operate the gate components. Each of these market categories plays a crucial role in the functionality of powered gates.</p></p>
