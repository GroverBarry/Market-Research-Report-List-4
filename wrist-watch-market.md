<p><p>The wristwatch market consists of two main types: mechanical watches and electronic watches. Mechanical watches operate using intricate gears and springs that are wound either manually or automatically, providing a classic and traditional timekeeping experience. Electronic watches, on the other hand, use quartz crystal technology to keep time with exceptional accuracy and often feature additional functionalities such as alarms, timers, and digital displays. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with mechanical watches appealing to traditionalists and collectors, while electronic watches cater to those seeking convenience and modern features.</p></p>
