<p><p>Ambulance vehicles market is divided into four main types: Type I, Type II, Type III, and Type IV ambulances. Type I ambulances are built on a truck chassis and are primarily used for transporting patients over long distances. Type II ambulances are van-based and used for transporting stable patients. Type III ambulances are built on a cutaway van chassis and are suitable for advanced life support services. Type IV ambulances are smaller vehicles used for quick response or transporting non-emergency patients.</p></p>
