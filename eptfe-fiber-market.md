<p><p>The ePTFE fiber market is segmented into three main types: membrane, sheet, and others. Membrane ePTFE fibers are widely used in various applications such as filtration, protective clothing, and medical textiles due to their high durability and breathability. Sheet ePTFE fibers are typically used for sealing applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction. The "Others" category includes specialty ePTFE fibers used in niche applications such as electrical insulation, outdoor gear, and chemical processing. Each type of ePTFE fiber offers unique properties and benefits for different industries.</p></p>
