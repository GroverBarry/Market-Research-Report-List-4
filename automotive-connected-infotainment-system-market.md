<p><ul><li>Embedded Systems</li><li>Smartphone Integrated Systems</li><li>Tethered System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive connected infotainment systems can be categorized into three main types: embedded systems, smartphone integrated systems, and tethered systems. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle, providing features such as navigation and entertainment without needing a separate device. Smartphone integrated systems allow users to connect their smartphones to the car's system for added functionality. Tethered systems involve using a device such as a tablet or portable media player to access infotainment features in the car. Each type offers different levels of integration and convenience for users.</p></p>
