<p><p>Explosion Relief Doors are safety devices designed to release pressure during explosions to prevent structural damage and protect personnel. There are two main types- Bottom Hinged and Top Hinged Explosion Relief Doors. Bottom Hinged doors swing open at the bottom, allowing pressure to escape upwards, while Top Hinged doors swing open at the top, allowing pressure to escape downwards. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings, such as refineries, chemical plants, and manufacturing facilities, to mitigate the impact of explosions.</p></p>
