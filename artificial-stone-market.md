<p><p>Artificial stone market includes various types such as cement artificial stone, polyester artificial stone, composite artificial stone, and sintered artificial stone. Cement artificial stone is made by mixing cement, aggregates, and pigments. Polyester artificial stone is manufactured using synthetic resin and fillers. Composite artificial stone is a combination of natural stone aggregates and resin. Sintered artificial stone is produced through a high-tech process involving extreme heat and pressure. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in the construction and design industries.</p></p>
