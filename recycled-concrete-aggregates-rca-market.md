<p><p>Recycled Concrete Aggregates (RCA) can be categorized into different types based on their source material. Mining Waste Based RCA is derived from crushed concrete waste generated during construction activities. Slag Based RCA is produced from industrial by-products like blast furnace slag. Ash Based RCA comes from coal combustion residues. Other types of RCA may include recycled glass or plastic aggregates. Each type of RCA offers unique properties and benefits for construction applications, promoting sustainability and reducing the environmental impact of traditional aggregates.</p></p>
