<p><p>Airline filter element market offers various types like photocatalyst, activated carbon, electrostatic precipitator, negative ions, ozone, and others. Photocatalyst filters use light energy to break down pollutants. Activated carbon filters absorb pollutants through a highly porous surface. Electrostatic precipitators use an electrical charge to attract and trap particles. Negative ion filters release negatively charged ions to neutralize pollutants. Ozone filters generate and release ozone to remove pollutants. Other types of filters may utilize different mechanisms to purify the air.</p></p>
