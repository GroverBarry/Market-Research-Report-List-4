<p><p>Semiconductor Dielectric Etching Equipment (SDEE) market is divided into two main types: wet etching equipment and dry etching equipment. Wet etching equipment involves using liquid chemicals to remove material from a semiconductor surface. On the other hand, dry etching equipment uses gas-phase or plasma-based etching techniques to achieve the same result without the use of liquids. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process, with each offering specific advantages and limitations for different applications.</p></p>
