<p><p>Extrusion coating is a process where a molten polymer is extruded onto a substrate to provide a protective barrier. The three main types of polymers used in extrusion coating are LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), EVA (Ethylene-Vinyl Acetate), and PP (Polypropylene). LDPE is known for its flexibility and transparency, making it ideal for flexible packaging. EVA offers good adhesion properties and is commonly used for food packaging. PP is known for its high heat resistance and barrier properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
