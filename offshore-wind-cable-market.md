<p><ul><li>Inter-Array Cable</li><li>Export Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore wind cable market consists of two main types: inter-array cables and export cables. Inter-array cables connect individual wind turbines within an offshore wind farm to a main substation, facilitating electricity transmission. Export cables, on the other hand, transfer the generated electricity from the main substation to the onshore grid. Both types of cables play a crucial role in the efficient transmission of renewable energy produced by offshore wind farms to the mainland.</p></p>
