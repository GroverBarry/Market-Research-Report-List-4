<p><p>Eternal Cavity Diode Lasers, or ECDLs, are a type of diode laser that utilizes an external cavity to achieve tunability and mode-locking capabilities. Tunable External-cavity Diode Lasers allow for precise control over the output wavelength, making them ideal for applications such as spectroscopy and telecommunications. Mode-locked External-cavity Diode Lasers produce ultra-short pulses of light, enabling high-speed data transmission and advanced imaging techniques. Both types of ECDLs play a crucial role in a variety of industries that require highly precise and controllable laser sources.</p></p>
