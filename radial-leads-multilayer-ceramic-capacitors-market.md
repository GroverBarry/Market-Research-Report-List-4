<p><p>Radial lead multilayer ceramic capacitors are available in various types, including NPO (COG), X7R, Y5V, Z5U, and others. NPO (COG) capacitors offer excellent temperature stability and low losses, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. X7R capacitors have a high capacitance value and are suitable for general-purpose applications. Y5V capacitors have a large capacitance range but lower stability over temperature. Z5U capacitors offer high capacitance and are commonly used in bypass and decoupling applications. Other types of capacitors cater to specific requirements in different electronic devices.</p></p>
