<p><p>Heavy axles market can be divided into two main types: Drive axles and Non-driven axles. Drive axles are responsible for delivering power from the engine to the wheels, allowing the vehicle to move forward or backward. Non-driven axles, on the other hand, are not connected to the engine and do not provide power to the wheels. They are primarily used for support and stability, particularly in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses. Both types play crucial roles in the overall performance and functionality of heavy vehicles.</p></p>
