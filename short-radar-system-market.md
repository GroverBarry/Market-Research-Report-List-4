<p><strong>The Short Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CW Radars, or continuous wave radars, emit a continuous signal and receive continuous reflections from targets. They are commonly used for applications like speed detection, distance measurement, and tracking moving objects. </p><p>Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, emit short bursts of radio frequency energy and measure the time it takes for the signal to bounce back from a target. Pulsed radars are widely used in applications that require high accuracy and resolution, such as weather forecasting, air traffic control, and military tracking systems.</p></p>
