<p><p>MDO-PE films are typically classified into two main market types: blown films and cast films. Blown films are made by blowing hot plastic through a circular die, creating a thin film that is strong and flexible. Cast films, on the other hand, are made by pouring molten plastic onto a flat surface and then cooling it to form a film. Each type of film has its own set of advantages and applications, with blown films often being used for packaging and cast films for industrial applications.</p></p>
