<p><p>The marine bilge pump market consists of two main types: electric bilge pumps and manual bilge pumps. Electric bilge pumps are powered by a motor and automatically remove water from the boat's bilge. They are convenient and efficient, but rely on a power source. Manual bilge pumps are operated by hand and are essential as a backup in case of power failure or when dealing with small amounts of water. Both types serve the important function of keeping boats dry and safe.</p></p>
