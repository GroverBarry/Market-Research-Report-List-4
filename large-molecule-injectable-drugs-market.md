<p><p>Large molecule injectable drugs market is categorized into three main types based on the mode of administration: intravenous injection, muscle injection, and subcutaneous injection. Intravenous injection involves delivering the drug directly into the bloodstream, allowing for rapid absorption and immediate effect. Muscle injection targets deep muscle tissues, providing sustained release of the drug. Subcutaneous injection involves administering the drug into the layer of fat below the skin, offering a slower and more prolonged release. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the drug and patient needs.</p></p>
