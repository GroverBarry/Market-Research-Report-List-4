<p><ul><li>Milling/drilling workholding</li><li>Turning workholding</li><li>Grinding workholding</li><li>EDM workholding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Workholding is essential in various machining processes to secure the workpiece in place during operation. In the milling/drilling workholding market, devices like vises and clamps are used to hold the workpiece securely. In the turning workholding market, chucks and collets are commonly employed to grip the workpiece. In the grinding workholding market, magnetic chucks and fixtures are utilized to hold the workpiece during grinding operations. In the EDM workholding market, specialized fixtures and clamping systems are utilized to securely hold the workpiece in place during electrical discharge machining.</p></p>
