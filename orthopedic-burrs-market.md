<p><p>Orthopedic burrs are specialized tools used in orthopedic surgery for shaping, drilling, or removing bone during procedures. There are four main types of orthopedic burrs: Wedge burrs, which have a sharp edge for precise cutting; Cylindrical burrs, which are used for drilling holes in bone; Conical burrs, which taper to a point for detailed work; and Spherical burrs, which are rounded for smoothing bone surfaces. Each type of burr serves a specific purpose in orthopedic surgery, providing surgeons with versatile options for bone preparation.</p></p>
