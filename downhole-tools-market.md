<p><p>The Downhole Tools market is segmented into various types, including Packer Class, Control Tool Class, Workover Tools, and others. Packer Class tools are used for sealing off specific zones in a wellbore. Control Tool Class tools are employed for controlling the flow or pressure within the well. Workover Tools are utilized for maintenance and reworking of existing wells. The "Other" market segment includes a wide range of downhole tools that do not fall into the aforementioned categories, such as fishing tools, drilling jars, and circulation tools.</p></p>
