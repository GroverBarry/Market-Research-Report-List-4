<p><p>The meat analyzer market can be broadly categorized into three main types: DEXA technology, NIR technology, and other technologies. DEXA technology uses dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry to determine the composition of meat based on its density. NIR technology, on the other hand, uses near-infrared spectroscopy to analyze the chemical composition of meat. Other technologies in the market include impedance technology, microwave technology, and ultrasound technology. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages and can be used in various applications within the meat industry.</p></p>
