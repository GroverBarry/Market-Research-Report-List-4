<p><ul><li>Complete Mix</li><li>Dough-Base Mix</li><li>Dough Concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Premix flour is available in three main types: complete mix, dough-base mix, and dough concentrates. Complete mix includes all the necessary ingredients for a specific baked product, making it convenient for bakers. Dough-base mix contains the dry ingredients, requiring only the addition of liquids by the baker. Dough concentrates are similar to dough-base mix but with a higher concentration of dry ingredients, providing more flexibility for customization. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the baking industry.</p></p>
