<p><p>Stainless steel forgings market comprises of three main types - castings, hot/cold forged parts, and sintered parts. Castings are produced by pouring molten metal into a mold to form the desired shape. Hot forging involves heating the metal and shaping it through mechanical force, while cold forging is done at room temperature. Sintered parts are made by compacting and heating metal powders without melting them. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of strength, durability, and cost-effectiveness for various industrial applications.</p></p>
