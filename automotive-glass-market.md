<p><p>Automotive glass market is segmented into three main types - laminated glass, tempered glass, and special function glass. Laminated glass is made up of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral in between, providing added safety by not shattering on impact. Tempered glass goes through a process of heating and rapid cooling, making it stronger and able to withstand high impact forces. Special function glass includes features like infrared protection, acoustic insulation, and self-cleaning capabilities, catering to specific consumer needs.</p></p>
