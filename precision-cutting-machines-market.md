<p><ul><li>Semiautomatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision cutting machines can be classified into two main types: semiautomatic and automatic. Semiautomatic machines require some manual input from the operator, such as setting parameters and loading materials, while the actual cutting process is automated. On the other hand, automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, making them more efficient and suitable for high-volume production. Both types of machines offer precision cutting capabilities, but automatic machines are typically preferred for larger scale operations due to their higher level of automation.</p></p>
