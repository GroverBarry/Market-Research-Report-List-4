<p><p>OLED emissive layer materials market includes several types such as hole transport layer (HTL) material, electron transport layer (ETL) material, and others. HTL materials facilitate the movement of positive charges in organic light-emitting diodes, while ETL materials help in the movement of negative charges. Other materials may include host materials, dopants, and charge generation materials. These materials play a crucial role in the functioning of OLED displays by efficiently transporting charges and generating light emission.</p></p>
