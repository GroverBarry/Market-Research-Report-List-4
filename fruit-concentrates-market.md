<p><p>Fruit concentrates market includes two main types: liquid concentrate and powder concentrate. Liquid concentrates are made by extracting water from the fruit juice, resulting in a more concentrated form. They are commonly used in beverages and food products. Powder concentrates are made by evaporating the liquid from the fruit juice until only a dry powder remains. They are known for their longer shelf life and easier storage and transportation. Both types are popular choices for adding flavor and sweetness to various food and beverage products.</p></p>
