<p><p>Bench-top bilirubin meters are stationary devices used in clinical settings to measure bilirubin levels in blood samples. On the other hand, transcutaneous bilirubin meters are portable devices that measure bilirubin levels through the skin without the need for blood samples. These meters are commonly used in pediatric clinics and hospitals for quick and non-invasive monitoring of bilirubin levels in infants.</p></p>
