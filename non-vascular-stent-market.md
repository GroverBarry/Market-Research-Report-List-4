<p><p>Non-vascular stents are categorized into various types based on the area of the body they are used in. Urinary tract stents are used to treat blockages in the ureter or kidney. Gastrointestinal stents help in treating blockages in the digestive system. Airway stents are used in the respiratory system to assist with breathing. Biliary stents are used in the bile ducts to treat obstructions. Pancreatic stents are used in the pancreas to help with drainage. Each of these stent types serves a specific medical purpose in its respective area of the body.</p></p>
