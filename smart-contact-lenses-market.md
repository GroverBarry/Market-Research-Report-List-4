<p><p>Smart contact lenses are available in two main market types - disposable and frequent replacement. Disposable smart contact lenses are designed to be worn for a short period of time, typically one day, before being thrown away. They are convenient and hygienic, but can be more expensive in the long run. Frequent replacement lenses are designed to be worn for a longer period of time, usually around two weeks or one month, before needing to be replaced.
