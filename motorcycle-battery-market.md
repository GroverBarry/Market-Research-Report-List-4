<p><ul><li>SLI</li><li>AGM</li><li>Lithium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle battery market comprises three main types: SLI (Starting, Lighting, Ignition) batteries, AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, and Lithium batteries. SLI batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries commonly used for starting motorcycles. AGM batteries are advanced maintenance-free batteries that provide superior performance and longer lifespan. Lithium batteries are lightweight, high-performance batteries that are becoming increasingly popular due to their durability and quick charging capabilities. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences in the motorcycle battery market.</p></p>
