<p><p>LVDT Linear Position Sensor market primarily consists of two types: Non-contact Position Sensors and Contact Position Sensors. Non-contact Position Sensors use technology such as magnetic fields or optical sensors to measure position without physical contact with the object being measured. This type is often used in applications where precision and reliability are crucial. On the other hand, Contact Position Sensors come into physical contact with the object to measure its position, making them suitable for applications where ruggedness and durability are key.</p></p>
