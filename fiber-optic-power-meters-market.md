<p><p>Fiber optic power meters are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their features and capabilities. Type I power meters are basic models designed for simple power measurements in fiber optic networks. They offer essential functions and are suitable for general use. On the other hand, Type II power meters are advanced models with additional features such as higher accuracy, multiple wavelength measurements, and compatibility with various connector types. They cater to more sophisticated applications and provide precise measurements in complex fiber optic systems.</p></p>
