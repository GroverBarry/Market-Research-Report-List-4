<p><p>Automatic Electrolyte Analyzers are devices used to measure the concentration of specific ions in a patient's blood or urine. There are two main types of these analyzers available in the market - Electrolyte Ion analyzers and Electrolyte pH analyzers. Electrolyte Ion analyzers measure the levels of specific ions such as sodium, potassium, and chloride, whereas Electrolyte pH analyzers measure both ion concentrations and pH levels in biological fluids. These analyzers play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring patients with electrolyte imbalances or acid-base disorders.</p></p>
