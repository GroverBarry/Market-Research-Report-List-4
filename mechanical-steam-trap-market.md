<p><strong>The Mechanical Steam Trap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float type (Lever Float and Free Float)</li><li>Inverted Bucket type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical steam traps are commonly categorized into float type and inverted bucket type. Float type traps include lever float and free float models, both utilizing buoyant mechanisms to open and close the trap based on the level of condensate. Inverted bucket traps, on the other hand, use a unique design where an inverted bucket rises and falls in response to the steam and condensate flow. Each type offers unique benefits and advantages in various steam system applications.</p></p>
