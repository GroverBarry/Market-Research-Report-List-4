<p><p>Automated Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) and Detection Sensor market types include radar, video, loop detection, infrared, multi-sensors, and others. Radar sensors use radio waves to detect vehicles and capture number plates. Video sensors use cameras to capture and process images for number plate recognition. Loop detectors are placed on the road to detect cars passing over them. Infrared sensors use infrared light to detect vehicles. Multi-sensors combine different technologies for enhanced detection capabilities. Other types of sensors may include ultrasonic sensors or microwave sensors for ANPR and detection applications.</p></p>
