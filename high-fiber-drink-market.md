<p><p>High fiber drinks can be classified into two main types: soluble fiber drinks and insoluble fiber drinks. Soluble fiber drinks contain fiber that dissolves in water, forming a gel-like substance that helps regulate cholesterol levels and blood sugar. On the other hand, insoluble fiber drinks contain fiber that does not dissolve in water and helps promote healthy digestion by adding bulk to stool. Both types of high fiber drinks are beneficial for overall health and can be incorporated into a balanced diet to support digestion and nutrient absorption.</p></p>
