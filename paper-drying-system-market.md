<p><p>Paper drying systems play a crucial role in the paper manufacturing industry. Rolling bed dryers utilize a continuous drying process, while air dryers use hot air to dry the paper. Convection dryers use forced air circulation to remove moisture, whereas conduction dryers use direct contact with heated surfaces. Other types of drying systems include microwave dryers and infrared dryers. Each system offers unique benefits and is suitable for different paper drying applications based on efficiency, capacity, and specific requirements of the paper manufacturing process.</p></p>
