<p><p>Lawn sweeper market can be broadly categorized into two types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are operated manually by pushing them across the lawn to gather debris and leaves into a collection bag or hopper. On the other hand, tow-behind sweepers are attached to lawn tractors or ATVs and are pulled along to efficiently cover larger areas. Both types of sweepers are designed to make lawn maintenance easier and more efficient for homeowners and landscapers alike.</p></p>
