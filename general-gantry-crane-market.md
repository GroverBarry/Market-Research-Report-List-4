<p><strong>The General Gantry Crane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Main Girder</li><li>Double Main Girder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General Gantry Crane market can be categorized into two main types based on their structure - Single Main Girder and Double Main Girder. Single Main Girder gantry cranes have one girder running along the length of the crane, providing support for the hoist and trolley. On the other hand, Double Main Girder gantry cranes have two girders running parallel to each other, offering increased stability and lifting capacity. Both types cater to different lifting requirements and are commonly used in various industries for material handling purposes.</p></p>
