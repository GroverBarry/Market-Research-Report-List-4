Nonionic surfactants are known for their mildness and compatibility with various ingredients, making them suitable for sensitive skin products. Amphoteric surfactants are versatile and can function in both acidic and alkaline environments. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are effective in conditioning and softening properties.
