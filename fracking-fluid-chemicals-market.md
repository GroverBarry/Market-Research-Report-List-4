<p><p>Fracking fluid and chemicals are used in hydraulic fracturing operations to extract oil and gas from underground reservoirs. There are three main types of fracking fluids based on their composition: water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used, while oil-gel based fluids are used in high-temperature and high-pressure environments. Foam-based fluids are used in formations where water-based fluids may not be effective. Each type of fracking fluid has unique properties and is selected based on the specific geology of the well.</p></p>
