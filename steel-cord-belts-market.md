<p><strong>The Steel Cord belts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strand Steel Cord Conveyor Belts</li><li>Layer Steel Cord Conveyor Belts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strand Steel Cord Conveyor Belts are constructed with individual steel cords twisted together to form a strong, durable belt. These belts are well-suited for heavy-duty applications where high tension and long distances are required. Layer Steel Cord Conveyor Belts are composed of multiple layers of steel cords embedded within the belt material. These belts offer excellent flexibility and impact resistance, making them suitable for applications where bending and folding are necessary. Both types of belts are commonly used in industries such as mining, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
