<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Surge Protective Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type 1 (Cope Direct Strike)</li><li>Type 2 (Limits Over Voltages)</li><li>Type 1+2 (Complete Protection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photovoltaic Surge Protective Devices are categorized into three main types based on their capabilities. Type 1 devices, also known as Cope Direct Strike, provide protection against direct lightning strikes. Type 2 devices, on the other hand, limit overvoltages and prevent damage caused by indirect lightning strikes. Type 1+2 devices offer complete protection by combining features of both Type 1 and Type 2 devices, ensuring comprehensive protection against all types of electrical surges in photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
