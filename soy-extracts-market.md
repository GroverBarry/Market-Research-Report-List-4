Soy protein concentrate is a high-protein powder extracted from soybeans, while soy protein isolate is a purer form of soy protein with a higher protein content. Textured soy protein is a meat substitute made from defatted soy flour, and soy flour is a product made by grinding soybeans into a fine powder. Each type serves as a valuable ingredient in various food products due to its nutritional benefits and versatility.</p></p>
