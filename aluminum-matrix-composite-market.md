<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Discontinuous</li><li>Particle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum Matrix Composite (AMC) in the market can be classified into three types: continuous, discontinuous, and particle. Continuous AMC consists of continuous reinforcement fibers, providing high strength and stiffness. Discontinuous AMC involves short fibers or whiskers randomly distributed in the matrix, offering improved toughness. Particle-reinforced AMC includes dispersed particles within the matrix, enhancing wear resistance and thermal conductivity. Each type has its unique properties and applications, catering to different industry needs for lightweight and high-performance materials.</p></p>
