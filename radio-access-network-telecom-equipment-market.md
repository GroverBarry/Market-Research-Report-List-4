<p><p>The Radio Access Network Telecom Equipment market consists of four main types of stations: Macro, Micro, Pico, and Femto. Macro stations provide coverage over a larger area and serve as the primary backbone of a network. Micro stations cover a smaller area and are used to enhance capacity in high-traffic areas. Pico stations are even smaller and are typically used indoors to boost coverage in specific locations. Femto stations are the smallest and are designed for use in residential or small business environments to improve signal strength and coverage.</p></p>
