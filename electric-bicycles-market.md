<p><p>Electric bicycles can be categorized into two main types: Brush electric bicycles and Brushless electric bicycles. Brush electric bicycles use brushes to transfer power from the battery to the motor, making them simpler and more affordable but also less efficient and requiring more maintenance. On the other hand, Brushless electric bicycles use electronic controllers to power the motor, resulting in a more efficient and reliable system with less maintenance. Overall, Brushless electric bicycles are considered to be the more advanced and preferable option in the market.</p></p>
