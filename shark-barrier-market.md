<p><p>Physical barriers are physical structures in the water that physically prevent sharks from entering certain areas. These barriers are often made of nets, fences, or underwater walls. Electronic barriers use technology such as underwater sensors or electrical pulses to create a deterrent for sharks. Both types of barriers are used in the market for protecting beaches, swimmers, and surfers from shark attacks. While physical barriers provide a more tangible form of protection, electronic barriers offer a more high-tech and potentially less intrusive solution.</p></p>
