<p><p>Household atomizers come in various types, including pneumatic atomizers, ultrasonic atomizers, and net atomizers. Pneumatic atomizers use compressed air to produce a fine mist of liquid, while ultrasonic atomizers use high-frequency vibrations to break liquid into tiny droplets. Net atomizers rely on a porous material to disperse liquid into the air. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers in the household market.</p></p>
