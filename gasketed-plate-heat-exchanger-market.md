<p><p>Gasketed Plate Heat Exchanger market can be classified into two main types: Single Circuit and Multi Circuit. In Single Circuit heat exchangers, the heating or cooling fluid flows in a single path through the plates, making them ideal for simpler applications. On the other hand, Multi Circuit heat exchangers have multiple paths for fluid flow, allowing for more complex and efficient heat transfer in larger systems. Both types offer various benefits and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
