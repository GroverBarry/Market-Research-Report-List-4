<p><p>A Food Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) System consists of various components such as Pulse Generator, Control Switch, Feed Pump, Processing System, and Cooling System. The Pulse Generator is responsible for generating short, high-intensity electric pulses. The Control Switch regulates the timing and frequency of these pulses. The Feed Pump delivers the food product into the Processing System where it is treated with the electric pulses. The Cooling System maintains the temperature of the product during and after processing.</p></p>
