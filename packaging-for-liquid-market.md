<p><p>Packaging for liquid products can be categorized into two main types: flexible and rigid. Flexible packaging includes options such as pouches, bags, and sachets that can easily adapt to the shape of the liquid. This type of packaging is usually more convenient for consumers and can be easily transported. On the other hand, rigid packaging includes bottles, jars, and cans that provide more protection and durability for the liquid inside. While flexible packaging is more common in industries like food and beverages, rigid packaging is often used for pharmaceuticals and household products.</p></p>
