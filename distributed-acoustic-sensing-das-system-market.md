<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing DAS System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) System market includes two main types: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing involves detecting changes in the speed of sound waves traveling parallel to the direction of wave propagation. Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing, on the other hand, detects changes in the speed of sound waves traveling perpendicular to the direction of wave propagation. Both types of sensing are used in various industries such as oil and gas, security, and infrastructure monitoring for real-time data collection and analysis.</p></p>
