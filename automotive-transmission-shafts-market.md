<p><p>Automotive transmission shafts come in two main types: Constant Velocity Joint (CVJ) transmission shafts and Lardan University Joint (UJ) transmission shafts. CVJ transmission shafts allow for smoother power transmission at varying angles, ideal for front-wheel drive vehicles. On the other hand, UJ transmission shafts are better suited for rear-wheel drive vehicles, providing strength and durability. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power transfer within the vehicle's transmission system, contributing to overall performance and reliability.</p></p>
