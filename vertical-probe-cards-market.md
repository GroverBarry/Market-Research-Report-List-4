<p><p>Vertical probe cards are essential tools used in semiconductor testing. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) vertical probe cards utilize tiny mechanical structures to make contact with the semiconductor device, providing high precision testing capabilities. On the other hand, non-MEMS vertical probe cards rely on traditional contact technologies for testing. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring proper functionality and quality control of semiconductor devices, with MEMS vertical probe cards offering higher accuracy and versatility in testing.</p></p>
