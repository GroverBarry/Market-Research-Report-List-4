<p><strong>The Pumps for Oil & Gas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pumps for the oil and gas market can be categorized into two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps use a rotating impeller to increase the pressure and flow of the fluid, making them suitable for applications with high flow rates. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, use a mechanism to trap and move a specific amount of fluid with each cycle, making them ideal for applications requiring precise flow control. Both types of pumps play critical roles in the oil and gas industry, serving different purposes based on their unique capabilities.</p></p>
