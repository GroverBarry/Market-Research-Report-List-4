<p><strong>The Mechanical Keyboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Switches</li><li>Tactile Non-Clicky Switches</li><li>Clicky Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical keyboards are categorized based on the type of switches they use. Linear switches provide smooth keypresses without any tactile feedback or clicking noise. Tactile non-clicky switches have a bump in the middle of the keypress, providing tactile feedback without the loud click. Clicky switches have both a tactile bump and an audible click sound when pressed. These different switch types cater to individual preferences for typing experience, allowing users to choose a keyboard that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
