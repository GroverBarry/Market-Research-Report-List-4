<p><p>The automotive rear view mirror market offers various types to cater to different needs. Flat mirrors provide a true reflection but have limited field of view. Convex mirrors offer a wider field of view by curving outward, reducing blind spots. Aspheric mirrors have a curved surface to minimize distortion and provide a wider angle. Wide angle mirrors further enhance visibility with an even broader perspective. Each type serves a specific purpose in improving safety and convenience while driving.</p></p>
