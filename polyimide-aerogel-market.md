<p><p>Polyimide aerogel can be categorized into three main types based on their density: Ultra Low Density, Low Density, and Other. </p><p>Ultra Low Density polyimide aerogels have the lowest density and are extremely lightweight, making them ideal for applications requiring maximum insulation with minimal weight. </p><p>Low Density polyimide aerogels are slightly denser than ultra-low density aerogels but still offer excellent thermal insulation properties. </p><p>Other types of polyimide aerogels cover a range of densities falling between ultra-low density and low density aerogels, providing a variety of insulation options for different industries and applications.</p></p>
