<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dot Type Temperature Sensor</li><li>Distributed Type Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dot Type Temperature Sensor is a type of optical fiber temperature sensor that measures temperature at a specific point using a sensing element at the tip of the fiber. Distributed Type Temperature Sensor, on the other hand, measures temperature continuously along the entire length of the fiber. Dot Type sensors are ideal for precise temperature measurements at specific locations, while Distributed Type sensors are suitable for monitoring temperature gradients and variations over larger areas or distances. Both types offer accurate and reliable temperature sensing capabilities for various industrial applications.</p></p>
