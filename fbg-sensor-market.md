<p><strong>The FBG Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point FPG</li><li>Distributed FPG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FBG sensor market can be divided into two main types: Point FBG and Distributed FBG sensors. Point FBG sensors consist of a single FBG element at a specific location, providing discrete measurements. On the other hand, Distributed FBG sensors have multiple FBG elements along the length of a fiber optic cable, allowing for continuous and real-time monitoring of large structures. Both types offer precise and reliable measurements for various applications ranging from structural health monitoring to industrial process control.</p></p>
