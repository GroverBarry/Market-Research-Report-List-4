<p><strong>The Mechanical CPR Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical CPR Devices market includes two main types: Electric and Pneumatic. Electric CPR devices run on electricity and use a motor to compress the chest, providing consistent compressions. Pneumatic CPR devices use compressed air to power the compressions, offering a portable and lightweight option. Both types aim to deliver high-quality and consistent chest compressions during CPR procedures. These devices are widely used in emergency situations where manual chest compressions may not be sustainable for an extended period.</p></p>
