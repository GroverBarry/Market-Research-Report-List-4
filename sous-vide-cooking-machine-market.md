<p><p>Sous Vide cooking machines come in two main types: immersion and water bath. Immersion types are compact devices that clip onto the side of a pot or container filled with water, heating and circulating the water to maintain a precise temperature for cooking. Water bath types are standalone machines with built-in water tanks that heat and circulate the water for cooking. Both types offer precise temperature control, making them popular choices among home cooks and professional chefs for creating perfectly cooked dishes.</p></p>
