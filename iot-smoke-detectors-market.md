<p><p>IOT smoke detectors come in three main types: Photoelectric, Ionization, and Combination. Photoelectric detectors use a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them ideal for slow, smoldering fires. Ionization detectors use radioactive particles to detect fast, flaming fires. Combination detectors combine both technologies for optimal detection of various types of fires. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, so it's important for consumers to choose the right type based on the specific needs of their home or business.</p></p>
